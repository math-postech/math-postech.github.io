Title: Cluster structures of Legendrian cablings

Abstract:

It is known that both augmentation varieties and moduli spaces of constructible sheaves admit A and X cluster structures, respectively, for a certain class of Legendrian knots and links. The first known such a class is a positive braid closure by Shen-Weng, which can be seen as a cabling (or a satellite) along the boundary of the Lagrangian disk properly embedded in the four-ball.

In the first part of the talk, we generalize this viewpoint to the class of cablings along the boundary of compact orientable surfaces L regarded as a Lagrangian in its cotangent bundle and consider algebraic and geometric objects associated with those Legendrians such as augmentation varieties AugL(Œ≤), moduli spaces of sheaves ShL(Œ≤), and so on. We show that both AugL(Œ≤) and ShL(Œ≤) admit cluster structures under the certain conditions,

In the second part, we consider a companion link S(Œª,Œ≤) of a positive braid closure Œª with the cabling data Œ≤. When Œª admits a Lagrangian filling L, then we discuss how AugL(Œ≤),Aug(S(Œª,Œ≤)) and Aug(Œª) (or ShL(Œ≤),Sh(S(Œª,Œ≤)) and Sh(Œª)), and their cluster structures are related to each other.

In particular, we also provide an algorithmic way in terms of N-graphs to describe the relationship between Lagrangian fillings of Œ≤ over L, and S(Œª,Œ≤),Œª over the disc.

This is a joint work in progress with Youngjin Bae(INU).
